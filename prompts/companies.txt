=========================================================
MODULE
=========================================================

CRM → Companies

The Company module represents businesses or organizations that your sales team is targeting or already working with.

Every company belongs to an Organization (Workspace).

A company can have multiple contacts, multiple leads, multiple activities, multiple notes, multiple attachments, and later multiple deals.

This module must be designed to scale like HubSpot, Salesforce, Attio, or Pipedrive.
